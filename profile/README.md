:wave: Welcome 

to, _Legal Knowledge Network_!

**What We Are Building:**

We are building a robust system that handles smart hyperlink UI/UX, pop-up cited text previews, split-screen reference views, and a Git-like "point-in-time" version control system to track legal amendments transparently. :rocket:

The platform digitizes and deeply structures legal texts (Constitutions, Bills, Acts), links them natively to public civic discussions, connects those discussions to verified political profiles, and powers the entire ecosystem with an ultra-fast semantic AI engine.

**The Mission:** 

The legal domain is incredibly complex, filled with dense jargon and confusing structures. In Nepal, navigating these laws is a massive challenge because documents are highly interconnected, scattering crucial context across hard-to-navigate cross-references. NIYAM is designed to solve this by creating a centralized, structured system that brings clarity and accessibility to legal texts for legal professionals, policymakers, and the general public.
